About LTU RKive

Welcome to LTU RKive, the official project repository for Lawrence Technological University. This platform serves as a hub for showcasing student and faculty research, projects, and academic contributions.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide an accessible and organized digital archive for preserving and sharing innovative work created at LTU. We believe that by documenting and showcasing these projects, we can foster collaboration, inspire future research, and celebrate academic excellence.
